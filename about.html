Mission

Provide easy-to-use, user-friendly, configurable and feature-rich accounting software that makes it super easy for small businesses to manage their billing, accounting, inventory & GST compliances. We are committed to offering cost-effective solutions that help businesses save time and money while ensuring compliance and accuracy.

Philosophy

Every business should have access to dependable and user-friendly accounting software, regardless of their scale. We will deliver affordable solutions that streamline biling, accounting, inventory management and tax compliance. We will back our product with unparalleled customer service, support and training programs that help our clients in increasing productivity and grow their business

Key Events in Our History

As we have grown over the years, we’ve helped thousands of businesses grow to their fullest. Here’s our story in a snapshot

1993
BUSY’s first version was introduced with Financial Accounting and Sales Tax Reports.
1994
Officially launched BUSY with Invoicing and Inventory features.
2000
The company transitioned to a Windows-based application, simplifying the product portfolio into three standardised categories.
2005
BUSY swiftly adapted to VAT in Delhi by introducing a VAT-Compliant Version.
2008
BUSY expanded its presence into the Middle East market.
2009
The company launched a pioneering Remote Access Edition, providing users with anytime, anywhere access to accounting data via the Internet.
2012
BUSY introduced BUSY 12, featuring enhanced functionalities like Quotation, Customizable Reports, SMS/E-mail Access, and AutoBack-up.
2014
BUSY 14 launched with new features including Triggers & Alerts (SMS/E-mail), Payroll, and Browser-based Data Access.
2016
BUSY 16 launched with a wide range of new business features, including Enquiries Management and Customer Support Management.
2017
BUSY 17, was released with GST features to help businesses in India comply with the new tax regime.
2018
BUSY 18 was unveiled with an Auto E-waybill Solution for Indian users, as well as an extensive VAT Solution designed specifically for GCC countries.
2019
BUSY introduced innovative and powerful in-built GSP features like Auto GSTR 1 / 2A / 2B / 3B Reconciliation and GSTIN / HSN Validation, etc.

GBS, Leading Software Solution provider for SMEs, serving more than 2500 Clients across Mumbai, deals in wide range of Software from Accounting, Taxation, Inventory, Payroll, HRMS and CRM Industry.
